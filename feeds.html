Task

Make a redesign of the main and internal pages of the news aggregator site in accordance with the new style

Role

UX/UI Designer

Company

NameOne

Analysis

Due to the fact that the task was only to update the design, the scratchy sketch stage and the flowcharts were skipped. First, I designed a logo and further developed materials had to match the style of the redesigned brand. The work on the site began with the construction of wireframes, but which I immediately put accents in corporate color, in order to understand how better to apply the elements of the new style without overloading the content area.

Homepage redesign

The redesigned homepage includes standard elements - after all, the siteâ€™s task remains the same - to provide news. However, the style has changed slightly, in accordance with the updated corporate identity. In the head area dominated by the main news slider - a standard element present in most of similar sites. Facebook group is very important, so the suitable widget was added to the layout; and a section with a list of news that is popular on Facebook follows the main news at once .

Each news is presented in the form of a card. To avoid unnecessary content uniformity, each latest news section has a notch on the corner of the card. This helps not only to visually separate headings, but also is a reference to the shape of the logo.

Check news on other sources

After clicking on the card with the news, the user enters the page with a short description and source of this news. Since the task of the site is to aggregate news, the user can read the full details only on the source site. On this site, he can read, share on social networks and check out this news on various sources.

Depending on the section of the selected news, the site offers the user a number of news from the same section.
